# Receptio

This formation is already being received by downstream organs in distinct ways.

ORGAN-II consumes the theory as visual substrate: curves, flows, and symbolic representations of consequence density.

ORGAN-III consumes the theory as product law: stake sizing, integrity asymmetry, audit thresholds, and verification boundaries. The live Commerce UI makes this reception visible rather than hypothetical.

ORGAN-IV consumes the theory as orchestration constraint: what may be routed, what may be simulated, and what must remain downstream truth rather than orchestration authority.

The most important reception test is not praise but fidelity. If downstream systems keep using the same terms to mean the same mechanisms, reception is healthy.
